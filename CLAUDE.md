# Athena — Design & Product Constitution

## Identity
Athena is a calm, intelligent investment decision assistant. Not a data dashboard. Not a trading platform. Not a news feed.

## Design Philosophy
Athena must feel like it was designed by an architect, not assembled by a developer.

Every design decision must pass this test:
- Does this increase clarity or trust?
- Does this make Athena more elegant or just more crowded?
- If it doesn't serve the user's decision — remove it.

## Design Principles
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Generous spacing
- Fewer but more meaningful elements
- Every section must have purpose
- No clutter. No noise. No unnecessary elements.

## The Feeling
When someone lands on Athena they must feel:
"I trust this."
"I feel comfortable here."
"I want to come back."

## Voice & Tone
- Calm and confident
- No hype language
- Short sentences
- Clear verdicts
- Analytical but human

## What Athena Is NOT
- Not a ticker feed
- Not a news aggregator
- Not a complex screener
- Not a broker
- Not overwhelming

## Core Value
One ticker. One verdict. Total clarity.

## The Standard
Athena must always feel:
Simple enough for a beginner.
Powerful enough for a professional.
Elegant enough to be memorable.
If a feature adds complexity without increasing clarity, it must be rejected.
